My favorite recipe - Chipotle Corn Salsa Recipe

  • 6 Ears Sweet Yellow Corn
  • 2 Poblano Chilies
  • 1/2 Red Onion
  • 2 Red Jalapenos
  • 1/3 Cup Chopped Cilantro
  • 1 Tablespoon Lime Juice
  • 1 Tablespoon Lime Juice

Salt and Pepper to Taste. Roast the corn and Poblano chilies on a grill or BBQ and let cool. Cut the corn off the cob. Dice the Poblano chilies, onion, and red jalapenos. Combine all the ingredients and season to taste.
